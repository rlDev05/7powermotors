## üìò Logo Concept & Design

### Primary Logo

The 7POWER MOTORS logo is a sophisticated blend of power, precision, and minimalist design philosophy.

#### Core Elements:

1. **The "7" Power Symbol**
   - Stylized number "7" integrated with a lightning bolt
   - Represents power, energy, and forward momentum
   - Enclosed in a subtle circular outline symbolizing completeness and precision
   - The negative space creates visual interest and depth

2. **Typography Treatment**
   - **"7POWER"**: Bold, uppercase in Rajdhani font
     - Font size: 1.5rem (24px)
     - Font weight: 700 (Bold)
     - Letter-spacing: 0.05em (expanded tracking)
     - Color: Foreground white (#f5f5f5)
   
   - **"MOTORS"**: Refined subtitle in Inter font
     - Font size: 0.5rem (8px)
     - Font weight: 500 (Medium)
     - Letter-spacing: 0.15em (wide tracking)
     - Color: Muted gray (#a3a3a3)

3. **Color Applications**
   - **Primary**: Matte black on light backgrounds
   - **Reversed**: White/silver on dark backgrounds
   - **Accent**: Metallic silver (#c0c0c0) for the power circle
   - **Monochrome**: All-white or all-black for specific applications

#### Logo Variations:

1. **Full Logo** (Default)
   - Icon + "7POWER MOTORS" text
   - Use for: Website header, marketing materials, signage

2. **Minimal Logo**
   - Icon + "7POWER" only (no "MOTORS" subtitle)
   - Use for: App icons, social media avatars, small spaces

3. **Icon Only**
   - Just the "7" power symbol in circle
   - Use for: Favicon, app icon, watermarks

## üéØ Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice Characteristics

1. **Confident**: We know motorcycles. We know riders.
2. **Refined**: Premium quality in every word
3. **Passionate**: We live and breathe motorcycles
4. **Direct**: Clear, honest, no-nonsense communication
5. **Sophisticated**: Educated tone without being pretentious

### Messaging Guidelines

**Do:**
- Use short, impactful statements
- Lead with benefits, not features
- Emphasize quality, performance, precision
- Speak to the emotion of riding
- Use active voice and strong verbs

**Don't:**
- Use overly technical jargon
- Make exaggerated claims
- Use flowery or poetic language
- Talk down to the audience
- Overcomplicate simple messages

### Example Headlines

‚úÖ **Good:**
- "RIDE THE POWER"
- "Minimal Design. Maximum Performance."
- "Engineered Perfection"
- "World-Class Brands. Unmatched Service."

‚ùå **Avoid:**
- "The Ultimate Motorcycle Experience of Your Dreams"
- "Feel the Wind in Your Hair as You Conquer the Road"
- "Revolutionary Next-Generation Advanced Technology"

## üñºÔ∏è Photography Style

### Image Guidelines

1. **Composition**
   - Clean, uncluttered backgrounds
   - Dramatic lighting (prefer dark, moody tones)
   - Sharp focus on the motorcycle
   - Minimal post-processing

2. **Color Treatment**
   - Slightly desaturated (not overly vibrant)
   - High contrast between subject and background
   - Consistent color grading across all images

3. **Subject Matter**
   - Premium motorcycles in pristine condition
   - Dynamic angles showing power and design
   - Lifestyle shots: riders in action, community events
   - Workshop/service: clean, professional environments

4. **What to Avoid**
   - Overly bright or cheerful imagery
   - Cluttered backgrounds
   - Low-quality or grainy images
   - Generic stock photos

## üé¨ Motion & Animation Principles

### Animation Philosophy

**Purpose Over Flash**
- Every animation must serve a purpose
- Never animate just for the sake of movement
- Subtle, elegant, professional

### Motion Characteristics

1. **Duration**: 300-800ms (never longer)
2. **Easing**: Smooth natural curves (easeOut, easeInOut)
3. **Properties**: Transform and opacity only (GPU-accelerated)
4. **Trigger**: User interaction or scroll-based reveal

### Common Patterns

```
Hover States
- Scale: 1.02-1.1
- Duration: 200-300ms
- Easing: easeOut

Scroll Reveals
- Fade in + slide up (20-40px)
- Duration: 600ms
- Delay: Stagger by 100ms per item

Navigation
- Slide in/out: 300ms
- Backdrop fade: 200ms
```
